How Our Services Are Delivered

Clear steps from first walkthrough to handover and ongoing AMC support.

1

Assessment & planning

Site visits, drawings or photos are reviewed to understand risks, cabling feasibility, device locations and integration needs.

2

Solution design & proposal

A clear BOQ is prepared with brand/model options, coverage or recording calculations and phasing possibilities where required.

3

Execution, handover & support

Installation, testing and user training are followed by AMC / on‑call support to keep systems healthy and up‑to‑date.
