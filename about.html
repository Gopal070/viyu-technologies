A hands-on security & automation integrator

VIYU TECHNOLOGIES LLP brings together CCTV, fire alarm, access control, PA, networking and home automation into one coherent, maintainable system for homes, campuses and workplaces.

Projects are driven by experienced engineers, project managers and technicians who understand how security, IT and electrical teams need to work together on real sites – not just in drawings.

Whether it is a single bungalow, an apartment tower, a school campus or a factory, VIYU’s priority is reliable coverage, neat execution and clear documentation so that systems keep working when needed most.

Meet the Founder

Not just a logo – there is a person who will actually pick up your call when a camera or panel goes down.

Founder & lead integrator

Praveen Kumar Singh, the person behind VIYU

After working on multiple CCTV, fire and access projects, Praveen Kumar Singh realised that the real gap was not in products, but in how they were designed, installed and handed over to the end user.

VIYU TECHNOLOGIES LLP was started to give clients one accountable partner – from walking the site and planning cable routes, to choosing the right mix of brands, to training guards and facility teams on how to actually use the system daily.

On most days, Praveen Kumar Singh is either on site, reviewing drawings, or experimenting with new technologies to keep solutions practical, secure and simple for non-technical users.

How we work

From walkthrough to working system

A clear, repeatable process so you always know what is happening on your project.

1

Site understanding

Joint walkthroughs, review of drawings and discussions around risks, blind spots, cabling routes, power points and existing infrastructure.

2

Solution design & BOQ

Multiple options where required, with clear BOQs, brand/model suggestions, coverage or recording calculations and integration with existing systems.

3

Execution & support

Neat installation, testing, handover training and AMC / health checks so that the system stays operational long after the project “completion” date.

Commitment to quality & safety

Every project follows checklists for design, installation and testing, aligned with good practices for electrical, fire and low-voltage systems.

Equipment is sourced from reputed manufacturers with proper warranties, and installed with correct accessories, protections and routing so that failures are minimised over the long run.

Customer-first delivery

Clients receive clear BOQs, wiring diagrams (where applicable), user training and basic SOPs so that day-to-day teams can operate systems confidently.

Post-handover, VIYU remains reachable – with AMC, health checks and remote support options – instead of disappearing once the last bill is raised.
