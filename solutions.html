Solutions

Application-based bundles for CCTV, fire, automation, PA, GPS and IT – designed around your site type instead of generic products.

How We Design Solutions

1. Understand the site & risks

Floor plans, photos or a quick visit are used to identify entry/exit points, critical rooms, outdoor areas and existing infrastructure.

2. Map products to use-cases

Appropriate cameras, detectors, speakers, locks and IT hardware are mapped to zones, keeping in mind budgets and standards.

3. Finalise bundle & phases

A practical phased BOQ (if needed) is shared, along with wiring approach, brand options and expected outcomes for each area.
